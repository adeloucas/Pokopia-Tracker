Dashboard

The left panel establishes your trainer profile, provides back-up and reset options for your data, and displays the app's credits.

Click through other tabs to the right of 'Dashboard' to access other parts of the app.

The right panel provides a searchable viewer of your Pokédex and Habitat completion.

Click any card to jump to see that pokémon or habitat's details.

Trainer name (optional) is saved with your data.

Export & Import Backup downloads / uploads your saved data as a JSON file.

Reset Tracker erases all data.

📖

Pokédex

The left panel displays a detailed, editable card view for each Pokémon when selected.
• Status is whether the Pokémon is acquired.
• Location is where the Pokémon is located.
• Planned is where the Pokémon is to be located based on household location (if different).
• Pref. Loc. is the canonical location for that Pokémon based on Habitat preferences.
• Ideal Hab. is the preferred habitat type for that Pokémon.
• Abilities are what the Pokémon can do.
• Habitats are where that Pokémon can be acquired.
• Navigation buttons at the bottom are cycle through the right panel's filtered content.

The right panel provides a searchable viewer of your Pokédex.
Click any card to see its detail in the left panel.
• Search and filter to find Pokémon based on Location, Specialties, and Ideal Habitats.
• Form Household lets you group acquired Pokémon into households.

Acquiring Pokémon — Click the ✓ ring to toggle acquisition.
Alternatively, use the button associated with the Pokémon's status in the detail view.

Placing Pokémon — When acquired, Pokémon can be placed in a location.
Location badges will appear on acquired cards: click to set that Pokémon's current location.
Alternatively, use the drop-down menu associated with the Pokémon's location in the detail view.

🏠 Form Household groups up to 4 acquired Pokémon, depending on house type.

🌿

Habitats

The left panel displays a detailed, editable card view for each habitat when selected.
• Description is flavor text.
• Pref. Location is the canonical location for that habitat based on soil or flora requirements.
• Attracts shows which Pokémon are drawn to this habitat.
• Navigation buttons at the bottom are cycle through the right panel's filtered content.

The right panel provides a searchable viewer of all habitats. Filter by location or ideal habitat type to narrow down results. Click any card to see its detail in the left panel.

When you click the "Mark as Complete" button in detail view or the circle on the habitat card, all associated Pokémon will be marked as acquired. This is irreversible, so use with caution!

Pokémon tags in detail view will change colors depending on their location.

When all Pokémon are acquired, the habitat will be considered complete.

Habitat states are:

FadedNo Pokémon acquired associated with that habitat.
Full colorAcquired some Pokémon, but not all.
Green ✓Acquired all associated Pokémon
🏠

Households

The left panel displays a detailed, editable card view for each household when selected. This is where you edit information as well.
• Name is how to search and identify the household.
• Type provides the household's 'house cost' associated with locations (max 40).
• Location is where the household is based. Note household member locations can differ!
• Navigation buttons at the bottom are cycle through the right panel's filtered content.

The right panel provides a searchable viewer. Filter by location or search by name to narrow down results. Click any card to see its detail in the left panel.

When you've acquired pokémon, you can group them into households if they are already not in one. Adding members provides ability and habitat information.

When a household location differs from a member's current location, a 'planned location' condition appears. This allows for you to plan where households and their members may be best moved between locations.

Household types determine the number of members that can be assigned and the associated costs.

Block Home0 slots · any members
Singles Prefab1 slot · 1 member max
Doubles / Quad2 slots · up to 4 members
📍

Locations

The left panel provides navigation between location details with immediate view of house slots. Click a location to open its details.

The right panel provides an organizational view of households and specialties.

Under a location's name is a count of what specialties and number of pokémon currently there, with a suggested canonical number labeled as 'local'.

Currently Available & Planned Organization toggle switches between current and planned location states of pokémon and households. This allows you to plan where to move acquired pokémon and households based on their current location, planned location, and the specialties of the location.

Pokémon and households will be catelogued under their specialties to help you see who is currently where, and plan where to move them to best fill location specialties.

When under the 'Planned Organization' view, you can see which specialties are missing between your planned organization and the location's 'local' population.

⚠️

Reset Tracker?

This will permanently erase all acquired Pokémon, locations, household data, and your trainer name. This cannot be undone.
